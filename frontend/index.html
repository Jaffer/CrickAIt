Help & FAQ

What is CrickAIt?

CrickAIt is your AI-powered companion for cricket scores, news, trivia, and statistics. It automatically tracks your favorite teams/players based on your conversations.

How do I add favorite teams/players?

Simply chat with the assistant! E.g. say "I support India" or "I love Virat Kohli", and it will silently save them to your Global Profile.

How does Pro Plan work?

Pro Plan unlocks our 70B expert LLM engine, giving you highly detailed tactics, stats tables, and matches predictions.
